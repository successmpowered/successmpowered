Team & Associates

Who you'd actually be working with.

Success Mpowered is a small, senior team of practitioners. Every engagement is led by a named partner. We bring in associates from our vetted faculty when the work calls for specific expertise.

Partners

Founding partners.

CG
Founder & Managing Partner

Christopher Greenwood

Christopher is Success Mpowered's founder and managing partner. He has spent over two decades working across multinational organisations and growth-stage businesses as a leadership consultant, advisor, and coach, with clients in the UK, India, and internationally.

His work sits at the intersection of leadership development and culture. He leads the firm's work with founders and CEOs, executive coaching engagements, and culture transformation partnerships, and is particularly interested in the leadership challenges of modern work: generational handovers, the pace of change, and the quiet pressures on leaders trying to do good work in those conditions.

AS
Partner

Aditi Sahai

Aditi is a partner at Success Mpowered. A qualified lawyer by background, she brings a rare combination to the firm's work: the analytical rigour of legal training, the operational discipline of running a global consultancy, and the human depth required for serious leadership and culture work.

She leads the firm's engagements across geographies and cultural contexts, and is the partner clients turn to when the work involves difficult organisational questions around fairness, inclusion, integrity, and the tricky human-and-structural dynamics that sit underneath culture.
