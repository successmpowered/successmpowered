Leadership Development

Leadership development that actually changes how people lead.

Cohort-based programmes for new managers, senior leaders, and executive teams. Built to develop the self-awareness, judgement, and interpersonal range modern leadership demands.

Who this is for

Built for organisations serious about the leaders they grow.

We work with organisations that have reached a point where leadership capacity is the constraint on what the business can do next — and where the usual answers (a one-off workshop, a generic online course, a new competency framework) aren't enough.

Our leadership work is right for you if:

  • You are promoting people faster than you are developing them.
  • Your managers were chosen for technical excellence and are now struggling with the human side of the role.
  • Your senior leaders are carrying the weight of change without the support to lead it well.
  • You have tried leadership training before and it didn't stick.
What a programme looks like

A programme, not a course.

A Success Mpowered leadership programme typically runs 4 to 9 months — combining live cohort workshops, one-to-one coaching, peer groups, and structured practice between sessions. We don't deliver content and disappear. We develop people over time, in community, with accountability.

01

Diagnose

We start by understanding your business, your leaders, and the leadership conditions they're operating in. Assessments, stakeholder conversations, and a clear design brief.

02

Design

We co-design the programme with your sponsor. Cohort size, themes, cadence, and success measures are shaped to your context.

03

Develop

Live workshops in-person and virtual. Small-group peer coaching. One-to-one coaching for each participant. Practice assignments between sessions. Ongoing sponsor touchpoints.

04

Embed

Manager debriefs, team-level application, and the cultural mechanisms that make the work stick after the programme ends.

Programme examples

A few shapes this work has taken.

New Manager

The New Manager Programme

For people who've recently stepped into managing others. Five months. 8–14 participants per cohort. Self-awareness, feedback, leading without authority, holding difficult conversations, managing across difference.

Senior Leader

The Senior Leader Intensive

For senior and C-suite leaders. Nine months. 6–10 participants. Leading through complexity, executive presence, decision quality, team dynamics at scale, leading change.

Executive Team

The Executive Team Programme

For an intact executive team, working together. Six to twelve months. Blended individual and team work. How this team decides, communicates, and leads the organisation as a collective.

What changes

What participants and their organisations notice.

For participants

  • Sharper self-awareness — they know their defaults, pressure responses, and range.
  • Better judgement under pressure. Fewer reactive decisions; more considered ones.
  • Interpersonal capability. Harder conversations happen; easier conversations go deeper.
  • A sense of being a leader, not just a senior operator.

For the organisation

  • Managers who retain their people.
  • Teams that can have disagreement without damage.
  • Leaders who model the culture the business says it wants.
  • A pipeline of people ready for the step-up the business needs next.
