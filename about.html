About

A consultancy built to do this work differently.

Success Mpowered was founded on a straightforward belief: organisations succeed when their people do. And people succeed when they are led well, in cultures that bring out their best.

Origin

Why we built it.

We founded Success Mpowered because the leadership and culture work we kept encountering in the market fell into two camps: big, glossy, and generic; or small, warm, and commercially shy. Neither was what our clients needed.

What they needed was a partner with the rigour of a consultancy and the human depth of the best coaches: someone who could move seamlessly between a boardroom conversation about strategy and a genuine developmental conversation with a senior leader who was struggling.

We built the firm to be that partner.

What we believe

Five convictions that shape the work.

01

Leadership is human before it is technical.

The capabilities that make the biggest difference in modern leadership are not the ones classic models rank highest.

02

Culture is a leadership output, not an HR project.

The culture you have is the sum of the behaviours your leaders model and tolerate.

03

Self-awareness is the highest-leverage skill.

A leader who knows themselves well leads with a different quality. Every hour spent here returns disproportionately.

04

Behaviour change is harder than people admit.

And worth doing anyway. Everything else is content.

05

Good work with good people matters.

It's more than a job. It is, for most of us, a large share of how we actually live.

Where we work

UK and India. Clients everywhere.

We are based in the UK and India, and work with clients internationally. Our cross-cultural reach is deliberate. It has shaped how we think, and it means we can support organisations whose leadership footprint crosses regions and contexts.
