# 03 — Recommended Sitemap

A premium consultancy site is small. Every page must earn its place. The recommended structure below favours depth over breadth.

---

## Top-level sitemap

```
HOME
│
├── WHAT WE DO
│   ├── Leadership Development
│   ├── Culture Transformation
│   ├── Executive Coaching
│   └── Team & Workshop Interventions
│
├── HOW WE WORK
│   └── Our Approach / Methodology
│
├── CLIENT WORK
│   ├── Case Studies
│   └── Client Outcomes
│
├── ABOUT
│   ├── Our Story
│   ├── Team & Associates
│   └── Our Philosophy
│
├── INSIGHTS
│   └── Articles, research notes, frameworks
│
└── CONTACT
    ├── Start a Conversation (qualifying intake)
    └── Office / Region info
```

---

## Primary navigation (what appears in the top nav bar)

A five-item top nav, with dropdowns where needed.

```
What We Do   |   How We Work   |   Client Work   |   Insights   |   About   |   [Start a Conversation →]
```

The CTA button *Start a Conversation* is the sixth visible item, styled distinctly.

## Pages to *remove* from the current site

- **Online Course** — unless it becomes a real revenue product, remove. Currently dilutes the consultancy positioning.
- **Empowered Well-being (as a top-level service)** — absorbed into Leadership/Resilience programmes or offered only to existing clients as an add-on.
- **Dedicated services page listing 9 topic icons** — replaced by four service pillars above.

---

## Pages to *add* that don't exist today

- **Case Studies** — the highest-leverage addition. Even 2–3 deep case studies transform the site.
- **Insights / Writing** — one article published monthly for 12 months builds a meaningful content asset.
- **Our Philosophy** — a place to hold the deeper, more reflective material (self-awareness, human nature, the nature of change) that doesn't belong on the homepage but gives the brand intellectual depth. This is where references to influences, lineage, and worldview can live respectfully.
- **Start a Conversation** — a qualifying intake page, not a generic contact form.

## Footer structure

The footer is where secondary navigation, legal, credentials, and contact details live. A well-designed consultancy footer is an underused trust-signal surface.

  Team & Workshop Interventions

Column 2 — About
  Our Story
  Team & Associates

  +44 7709 532849 (UK)
  +91 9619 102556 (India)
  LinkedIn → 

Bottom bar
  © Success Empowered 2026 | Privacy | Terms | Cookie preferences | Accreditations (logos)

The accreditations row (ICF / EMCC / CIPD / ILM or whichever are real) should appear as a small logo strip in the footer — a passive but permanent trust signal on every page.

## Mobile navigation

Full-screen overlay menu on mobile (not a small dropdown). Large tap targets. Same structure as desktop nav. CTA button persistent at the bottom of the menu.

## Content inventory & phasing

If the redesign must ship in phases, build in this order:

**Phase 1 — Ship within 6 weeks (the strategic minimum viable site):**
- Home
- Leadership Development
- Culture Transformation
- Our Approach
- About (Our Story + Team)
- Contact / Start a Conversation

**Phase 2 — Ship within 3 months:**
- Executive Coaching
- Team & Workshop Interventions
- 2–3 Case Studies
- Insights hub scaffold + 3 launch articles

**Phase 3 — Ongoing:**
- Additional case studies (target: 1 per quarter)
- Articles (target: 1 per month for 12 months)
- Our Philosophy (once the brand is settled enough to have a public thesis)

Don't launch Phase 1 without at least one proof artefact — either a named client logo row, one deep case study, or named outcome metrics. Launching without commercial proof locks in the problem the old site has.
