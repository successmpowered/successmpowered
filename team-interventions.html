What this covers

Three shapes of team work.

Off-site

Leadership team off-sites

Designed and facilitated two-day sessions for senior teams at an inflection point: new strategy, new members, post-acquisition, post-crisis.

Realignment

Team realignment

Focused work with a team that has stopped functioning well — conflict, drift, loss of purpose, misalignment with the wider organisation.

Series

Workshop series

A short series of sessions on a specific theme: psychological safety, feedback culture, navigating change, for a team or department.

What we don't do

No ice-breakers. No generic team-building. No pre-packaged decks.

We don't do one-off energiser workshops. We don't do generic team-building. We don't deliver pre-packaged content to rooms of strangers.

Every team engagement is designed for the team in the room. The design takes time. That's the work.

Real team development is rarely delivered by someone who met the team that morning.
How we work with teams

A shape you can count on.

01

Listening

Short conversations with the team leader, often one or two members, and relevant stakeholders. We develop a picture of what's actually happening.

02

Design

A bespoke session plan that reflects the team, the moment, and the outcomes the leader is actually trying to reach.

03

Facilitate

In-person or virtual. Senior facilitation, experienced with the kind of difficult conversations team development requires.

04

Follow-up

A structured debrief with the team leader and agreed next steps to embed what emerged.

If there's a team that needs focused, serious attention — let's talk about the shape it could take.
