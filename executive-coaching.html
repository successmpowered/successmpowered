How we coach

Deep work. Treated as such.

Depth of attention. No templates, no 50-question intake forms. The first session is a conversation. We'll know within two sessions whether this is the right fit.

Honest challenge. The point of good coaching is not to feel better. It is to lead better. We'll say the hard things, respectfully.

Rooted in practice. Sessions connect directly to what you're actually doing this week. The development happens in the work, not separate from it.

Strictly confidential. What's discussed in coaching stays in coaching. Where engagements are sponsored by a company, the coaching relationship itself remains private. Only development goals and agreed outcomes are shared.

Engagement shape

  • Typical engagement: 6 to 12 months.
  • Cadence: fortnightly sessions, 90 minutes.
  • Delivery: in-person where geography permits, otherwise high-quality video.
  • Between sessions: access to your coach for brief check-ins when something needs thinking about in real time.
